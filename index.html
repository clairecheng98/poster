Introduction

Ink traps, a strange feature for a typeface so informed by woodtype, are useful in optimizing both large and mid range optical sizes in different ways. In the mid to thinner widths they help maintain texture and usefullness at smaller sizes. In the tightly packed Black weight, they facilitated the hairline negative space the defines the letters.
